Control and monitor your home from anywhere in the world. GiToon Companion lets you control all your devices and provides advanced location and notification engines to allow for complex mobile automations.

GiToon is an open-source home automation platform that focuses on local control and privacy. Supported products include:

GiToon Companion requires an iPhone running iOS 10.3 or later and a configured GiToon instance. The instance must use GiToon Cloud or be accessible from the Internet and secured with a valid, non self-signed, SSL certificate for location tracking. Basic notifications work without external access.
